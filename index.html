👋 I research Human-Centered AI at Makeability Lab and Human-Centered Robotics at RAIVN Lab, advised by Prof. Jon Froehlich and mentored by Jae Lee and Jiafei Duan. My work focuses on designing and evaluating context-aware, multimodal, human-centered systems that provide personalized assistance for everyday tasks and assist various accessibility groups. Outside academia, I enjoy refining card magic routines, crafting cardistry flourishes, inventing Rubik's Cube algorithms, training calisthenics skills, and binge-watching podcasts.
