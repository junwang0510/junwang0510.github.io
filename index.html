👋🏻I received my B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Washington. I researched Human-Computer Interaction, Augmented Reality, and Artificial Intelligence under the guidance of Professors Jon Froehlich and Ranjay Krishna, mentored by Jaewook Lee and Jiafei Duan. My work emphasizes the design and evaluation of context-aware, multimodal, human-centered systems that deliver personalized assistance for all.

I greatly enjoy cardistry, card magic, calisthenics, cubing, chess, cognition, and cinema.

    CHI 2024 | [PDF] [Video] [UW News] [UW Undergraduate Research Symposium]
