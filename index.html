👋🏻I research Human-Centered AR and AI under the supervision of Prof. Jon Froehlich and Prof. Ranjay Krishna, mentored by Jaewook Lee and Jiafei Duan. My work focuses on designing and evaluating context-aware, multimodal, human-centered systems that provide personalized assistance for everyone.

Outside academia, I enjoy refining card magic routines, crafting cardistry flourishes, inventing Rubik's Cube algorithms, training calisthenics skills, and binge-watching podcasts.

Current affiliations: Stanford HAI, UW CSE, Makeability Lab, RAIVN Lab, UW XRA
